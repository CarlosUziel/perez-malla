Do you need to extract insights from your data?

I will evaluate your data analysis needs and find the best solution for your business or research goals. No over-complicated and expensive methods when simple statistics will get you what you want in a faster and more cost-effective way.

I am a PhD(c) in Bioinformatics and I hold both a M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence and a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering. I have more than 8 years of experience in data analysis and machine learning in different domains. I focus on the Python data stack, from Pandas and Numpy to PyTorch and Streamlit.

Services

When you decide to work with me, you can expect a thorough, reliable and responsive professional determined to drive your project forward. You will receive the personalized attention of an independent contractor. I will be your sole point of contact and be in charge of all the solutions tailored to your needs.

  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
