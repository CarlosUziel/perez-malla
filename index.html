Do you need to extract insights from your data?

I will evaluate your data analysis needs and find the best solution for your business or research goals. No over-complicated and expensive methods when simple statistics will get you what you want in a faster and more cost-effective way.

I hold a PhD in Bioinformatics, a M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence, a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering and a B.Sc. in Business Management. I have more than 8 years of experience in data analysis and machine learning in different domains, from computer vision and genomics datasets to language modelling. I focus on the Python data stack, from Pandas and Numpy to PyTorch and Streamlit.
